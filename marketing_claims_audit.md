# Marketing Claims Audit – VoiceFirst AI for Teachers

## Workshop – Rà soát AI Marketing Claims

**Sản phẩm:** VoiceFirst AI for Teachers – AI Voice Classroom Assistant Platform
**Phạm vi audit:** Website, pitch deck, demo script, onboarding materials, LMS integration pages, school procurement materials.

---

# Mục tiêu

Rà soát toàn bộ AI claims liên quan đến:

* Voice AI
* Classroom Automation
* LMS Integration
* AI Teaching Assistant
* Speech Recognition
* Attendance Automation
* Educational Productivity
* Privacy & Security

Nhằm:

* giảm legal/compliance risk
* tránh overclaiming
* tăng trust với trường học và doanh nghiệp giáo dục
* chuẩn bị cho procurement review và IT/security assessment

# Risk Classification

## 🟢 Mức A – Chứng minh được

Có:

* measurable latency
* production telemetry
* reproducible demo
* classroom pilot
* interaction logs

### Ví dụ tốt

* “Real-time voice interaction for supported classroom workflows.”
* “Average slide response latency under 1 second in pilot deployment.”

---

## 🟡 Mức B – Có khả năng đúng nhưng chưa đủ evidence

Có:

* internal benchmark
* pilot classroom deployment
* prototype validation
* limited-scale testing

### Ví dụ

* “Improves teaching productivity.”
* “Hands-free classroom interaction.”

---

## 🔴 Mức C – Overclaim / nguy hiểm

Các dấu hiệu:

* tuyệt đối hóa
* anthropomorphism
* impossible guarantees
* security guarantees
* automation guarantees
* privacy perfection wording

### Trigger words nguy hiểm

* “100%”
* “Never”
* “All”
* “Fully autonomous”
* “Zero latency”
* “No privacy risk”
* “Understands everything”

# Common AI Marketing Risks for Voice AI Products

## 1. Speech recognition ≠ perfect understanding

Voice AI phụ thuộc vào:

* microphone quality
* classroom noise
* accent variation
* internet latency
* speech overlap

=> Không nên claim perfect accuracy.

---

## 2. “AI classroom assistant” cần tránh overautomation wording

Vì:

* giáo viên vẫn chịu trách nhiệm chính
* AI chỉ hỗ trợ workflow
* classroom operations cần human oversight

=> tránh wording kiểu “fully autonomous classroom”.

---

## 3. LMS integration claims cần cụ thể

School IT teams sẽ hỏi:

* support LMS nào?
* có SSO không?
* có role-based access không?
* dữ liệu lưu ở đâu?
* có audit logging không?
* có FERPA / PDPL consideration không?

=> tránh claim “works with all LMS systems”.

---

## 4. Voice AI luôn có privacy concern

Đặc biệt trong giáo dục:

* voice recording
* student privacy
* classroom transcripts
* retention policy
* consent collection

=> không nên claim “privacy-safe by default”.

---

# Honest Rewrite Principles

## Tránh

* tuyệt đối hóa
* replacement wording
* impossible guarantees
* anthropomorphic wording
* security perfection wording

## Ưu tiên

* “helps”
* “supports”
* “designed to”
* “can reduce”
* “assists teachers”
* “for supported workflows”
* “with teacher oversight”

# Suggested Safe Messaging for VoiceFirst AI

## Safer positioning

### Instead of:

> “Fully autonomous AI classroom.”

### Use:

> “Voice AI designed to support classroom workflows with teacher oversight.”

---

### Instead of:

> “100% accurate classroom speech recognition.”

### Use:

> “Speech recognition performance depends on classroom conditions and hardware setup.”

---

### Instead of:

> “AI understands your classroom.”

### Use:

> “AI processes supported classroom voice interactions and commands.”

---

### Instead of:

> “Plug-and-play with every LMS.”

### Use:

> “Supports integration with selected LMS platforms and APIs.”

# Final Self-Check

## Key questions

### 1. Nếu bỏ overclaim:

* school buyers còn thấy value không?
* teachers còn hiểu use-case không?
* procurement còn approve dễ không?

### 2. Claims nào cần proof thêm?

* latency benchmark
* speech accuracy benchmark
* classroom pilot
* LMS integration testing
* privacy review

### 3. Claims nào nên remove hoàn toàn?

* “100% accurate”
* “fully autonomous”
* “zero privacy risk”
* “works everywhere”
* “replace teachers”

# Recommended Governance Process

## Before publishing any AI marketing material

### Checklist

* Có measurable evidence?
* Có reproducible demo?
* Có wording tuyệt đối không?
* Có implied guarantee không?
* Có privacy/security overclaim không?
* Có anthropomorphic wording không?

Nếu “YES” → cần rewrite.
